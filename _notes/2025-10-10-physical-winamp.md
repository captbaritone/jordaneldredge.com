Winamp’s default skin, and many of the community-made [custom skins](https://skins.webamp.org/?query=Stereo), used a [skeuomorphic](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Skeuomorph) design style, where a digital interface models a physical interface. Combined with Winamp’s strong [nostalgic emotional connection](https://jordaneldredge.com/blog/this-software-is-punk-rock/), it’s not surprising that many digital artists have explored the idea of **a Winamp you could slip in your pocket**.

As part of my [interest in Winamp](https://jordaneldredge.com/blog/winamp-skin-musuem/), I’ve made a habit of collecting these pieces as I came across them, and I figured now (before the internet is overrun with infinite AI versions) I’d collect them up as a little archive. I’ve tried to find attribution for piece but was often unsuccessful. If you have a source or correction for one of these, or have a piece you think should be included here, please [get in touch](https://jordaneldredge.com/contact/)!

Without further ado: Winamp!
